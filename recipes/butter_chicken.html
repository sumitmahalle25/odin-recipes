Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a rich and creamy Indian curry made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a spiced tomato gravy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Tomato puree
  • Butter
  • Cream
  • Garlic & ginger
  • Garam masala

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with spices.
  2. Cook chicken until tender.
  3. Prepare tomato-based gravy.
  4. Add cream and butter.
  5. Combine chicken with gravy and simmer.
